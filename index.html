Anyone can make their own sushi. We set up Sushi Coach based on this simple observation.

What we do

During the course of a convivial evening, Sushi Coach helps you prepare sushi yourself. After that, youâ€™ll know how to make sushi for your own pleasure or that of your guests!

What you'll learn

You get to choose 4 techniques and we'll coach you through each of them. After that, you'll have access to follow-up coaching videos on the Sushi Coach website.

We'll Come To Your Place

We cover the whole of Montreal Island and a few other places in the Greater Montreal area.


What's included

All ingredients and sauces as well as essential sushi-making utensils are provided. We can't supply drinks, but you can make great pairings with our suggested drinks list.

We love vegeterians

Menus and selections are adapted to your tastes. Sushi offers a lot of delicious and varied options for vegeterians too!

An Affordable Treat

A full evening of coaching costs $50 per person, all included. Children are welcome, and consequetly are only half price.
